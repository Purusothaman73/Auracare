Comprehensive Medical Assistance at Home

Recovery after surgery or a medical procedure requires specialized care to prevent complications and ensure a smooth healing process. At Aura Care, our trained nurses and caregivers provide comprehensive post-surgical and rehabilitation support in the comfort of your home. We help individuals regain their strength, mobility, and independence while ensuring proper medical supervision and emotional support.

Our Post-Surgical & Rehabilitation Care Services Include:

Wound Care & Dressing Changes

Proper wound care is crucial to prevent infections and promote healing. Our skilled nurses monitor surgical wounds, change dressings, and ensure the wound is healing correctly. We follow strict hygiene protocols and doctor-recommended wound care routines.

Pain Management & Medication Assistance

Managing post-surgical pain is essential for a comfortable recovery. Our caregivers provide medication reminders and assist with prescribed pain management techniques, such as cold/hot therapy, relaxation exercises, and position adjustments to alleviate discomfort..

Mobility Support & Physiotherapy Assistance

Regaining mobility is a key part of recovery. Our caregivers help with safe movement, encourage prescribed exercises, and assist in following physiotherapy routines to restore strength, flexibility, and coordination after surgery.

Catheter & Drain Care

For individuals with urinary catheters or surgical drains, our skilled nurses provide professional monitoring, cleaning, and management. We ensure hygiene, prevent infections, and educate patients and family members on proper care techniques.

Nutritional Support & Hydration Monitoring

A proper diet plays a crucial role in post-surgical healing. Our caregivers assist in preparing doctor-recommended meals, ensuring adequate hydration, and monitoring dietary intake to support a faster and healthier recovery..

Respiratory Support & Breathing Exercises

For patients recovering from lung-related surgeries or medical conditions, our team provides respiratory care, including breathing exercises, oxygen therapy support, and postural drainage techniques to improve lung function.

Bowel & Bladder Management

After surgery, patients may experience temporary or long-term changes in bowel and bladder function. Our caregivers assist with toileting, incontinence care, and maintaining hygiene while ensuring dignity and comfort.

Emotional & Psychological Support

Surgery and rehabilitation can be mentally and emotionally challenging. Our caregivers offer companionship, encouragement, and emotional reassurance to help patients stay positive and motivated throughout their recovery journey.

Doctor Coordination & Follow-Up Support

We assist with scheduling doctor’s appointments, organizing medical reports, and ensuring timely follow-ups to track recovery progress and make necessary adjustments to care plans.

  • How can home nursing care help with post-surgical recovery?
    Home nursing care provides personalized, round-the-clock support to help manage pain, monitor wound healing, prevent infections, and assist with mobility exercises. Our trained nurses ensure that recovery is smooth, reducing hospital readmission risks and improving overall well-being.
  • How long will I need post-surgical care at home?
    The duration of post-surgical care depends on the type of surgery, individual recovery speed, and doctor’s recommendations. Some patients may require care for a few weeks, while others with complex surgeries may need extended support for several months. Our team will assess your condition and create a customized care plan based on your needs.
  • Can your nurses assist with pain management and medication?
    Yes! Our skilled nurses closely monitor pain levels, provide medication reminders, and assist with doctor-prescribed pain relief techniques, including cold/hot therapy, relaxation exercises, and repositioning for comfort. We ensure that pain is managed effectively for a smoother recovery.
  • Do you offer physiotherapy support along with post-surgical care?
    While our caregivers assist with basic mobility support and prescribed exercises, we also coordinate with licensed physiotherapists if needed. We help patients follow their therapy plans, ensuring they regain strength, flexibility, and independence at a steady pace.
